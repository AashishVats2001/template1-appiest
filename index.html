What is Hexapay?

HexaPay is a next-generation multi-chain payment gateway built for the decentralized economy. We enable individuals, startups, and enterprises to send, receive, and manage crypto payments with unmatched speed and security — across all major blockchains.

Our platform bridges the gap between crypto and real-world usability by offering seamless integrations, developer-first APIs, and a powerful dashboard that makes crypto payments as intuitive as traditional methods.

Whether you're an online store, SaaS company, freelancer, or DeFi platform — HexaPay empowers you to accept payments globally, reduce fees, and transact with confidence.
